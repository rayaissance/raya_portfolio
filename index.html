Cake Website

This is a sweet fusion of culinary creativity and artistic expression. Specializing in gourmet cakes and custom desserts, we blend bold flavors with beautiful design to make every bite unforgettable. The project’s user interface was thoughtfully designed in Figma and implemented as a fully responsive website, delivering a seamless and visually rich experience for dessert lovers everywhere.

Coffee Inventory and Product Management

A solution for tracking coffee stock, managing product data, and optimizing inventory operations for cafés and small businesses. Designed with an intuitive interface in Figma and implemented as a responsive website, the system offers real-time inventory monitoring, product categorization, and simplified restocking workflows — all in one user-friendly platform.

Anime T-Shirt Design

Created as a design project for a small t-shirt brand that merges anime culture with streetwear style. Featuring the powerful and iconic Satoru Gojo, the artwork was crafted to be print-ready for apparel and promotional use, appealing to anime fans and fashion-forward audiences alike.

Game

Project Life Saver: First Aid Game is an interactive learning tool designed to teach essential first aid skills through engaging, game-based scenarios. Aimed at increasing emergency readiness for all ages, the game simulates real-life situations where quick thinking can save lives. The user interface was carefully crafted in Figma, focusing on accessibility, clarity, and a fun, educational experience.
